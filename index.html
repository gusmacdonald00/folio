Data sources
Where the data comes from

folio aggregates publicly available information across multiple regulatory and market sources. Australian investor activity is drawn from ASX market announcements, substantial shareholder notices (Form 603/604), fund manager quarterly updates, and NTA disclosures for listed investment companies. US fund manager positions are sourced from SEC Form 13-F filings, Form 4 insider disclosures, and Schedule 13G/13D beneficial ownership reports. ARK Invest positions are sourced from their daily trade notification feed. Asian investor activity is drawn from HKEX, TSE/JPX, SGX, NSE/BSE India, and Bursa Malaysia filings. Intraday price data refreshes every 15 seconds during market hours.
